CLIENT'S REVIEW

Robert

My mother needed emergency surgery, and the staff was incredibly professional and compassionate. The surgeons explained everything clearly, and the nursing team checked on her regularly, making sure she was comfortable. The facility was clean, and the administrative staff helped us navigate insurance with ease. We felt supported every step of the way.

Nancy

The doctor was knowledgeable and made me feel at ease, which I appreciated. However, the wait time in the emergency room was longer than expected—about three hours before I was seen. The staff was polite but seemed stretched thin. The room was clean, and the care I received was good, but I hope they can improve on wait times for future visits.

Gail

The nurses were attentive, always checking in with a smile, and even helped me stay calm when I was nervous about a procedure. The food was surprisingly decent, and the rooms were spotless. The only hiccup was a slight delay in discharge paperwork, but it wasn’t a big deal. I’d trust this hospital with my care any day!
